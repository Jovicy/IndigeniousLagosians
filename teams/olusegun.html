Niyi Kassim Bashua

Vice President 2

His father is the son of Anidu Kasumu-Bashua. His grandfathers lineage is from birth Bashua and Ashogbon. He married Safurat Iginla. His mother is from the Onikoyi's family and the grand daughter of Aluko Onikoyi (muti line) and from the Igbobi Sapee family. Niyi went to Ansaruddeen College Isolo. He is a graduate of Rutgers University in New Jersey. He is in the pharmaceutical industry and runs his own private business as well. He is a father of 3 beautiful children.
