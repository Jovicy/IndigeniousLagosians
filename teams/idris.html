Mr Idris Tele Olukoya

Strategic Director

Mr Idris Tele Olukoya hails from Ibonwon Okemagba Epe Division. He is the son of Late Chief Robiu Oloruntele Olukoya who was the former Director of Pilgrimage and Water Corporation Lagos State under Gov. Lateef Jakande Administration. Mr Olukoya is a graduate and masters holder from University of Lagos. He has also bagged many professional certifications in Education and management field. Mr Olukoya has contributed immensely towards community developments initiatives in Epe such as Epe got talent, Epe Fish Festival, Epe international Annual Trade Fair, Epe Division Community Annual Awards, Yearly Food Palliative for the vulnerable amongst many others. He brings on this project his passion, drive and dedication for sustainability development of Indigenous Lagosians as a whole.
