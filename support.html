Support Us

Welcome to the Indigenous Lagosians Forum! Your support means the world to us as we strive to preserve and celebrate the rich heritage of Lagos and its indigenous communities. Here are ways you can contribute:

  • Donation of Heritage Items: We welcome donations of significant heritage items that hold historical value for Lagosians. Whether it's traditional artifacts, cultural relics, vintage photographs, or documents that showcase the unique heritage of Lagos, your contributions help us preserve our cultural legacy for future generations.
  • Old Building Restoration: Preserving historical architecture is vital in maintaining the cultural fabric of Lagos. Your support through the donation or sponsorship of old buildings' restoration projects enables us to conserve these structures, turning them into living monuments that tell the story of our city's past.
  • Financial Support: Your financial contributions assist us in organizing events, workshops, and educational programs that promote the understanding and appreciation of Lagos' indigenous culture. Your donations directly aid our efforts in research, documentation, and community outreach initiatives.
  • Spread the Word: Help us broaden our reach by spreading awareness about our cause. Share our mission with your network, on social media, and among like-minded individuals who value the preservation and celebration of Lagos' indigenous heritage.
  • How to Donate To donate heritage items, old buildings, or contribute financially, please contact us through our provided email or phone number. We'll be delighted to discuss how your contribution can support our mission effectively

    • Thank you for your support in safeguarding and celebrating the rich cultural heritage of Lagos. Together, we can ensure that the legacy of our indigenous communities continues to thrive for generations to come.
